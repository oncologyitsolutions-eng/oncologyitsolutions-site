Background

I'm a healthcare IT professional with over 20 years of experience, including 17+ years focused specifically on Radiation Oncology and clinical systems. I've supported hospitals, medical universities, and private practices, serving as a primary support specialist for treatment planning and oncology EMR systems.

My work has spanned major software upgrades, system architecture redesigns, data center migrations, and full implementation projects — including leading an implementation that replaced a legacy treatment planning system with a seamless go-live. I've also worked as an independent consultant, helping oncology sites assess their systems and workflows and identify opportunities for improvement.

Outside of work, I build LEGO — and there's a real parallel to what I do professionally. Whether it's interlocking bricks or interlocking clinical systems, the satisfaction is the same: taking individual pieces and fitting them together into something whole that actually works.
